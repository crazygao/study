ambivalence
### Meaning
+ _noun_: The coexistence of opposing attitudes or feelings, such as love and hate, toward a person, object, or idea
+ _noun_: Uncertainty or indecisiveness as to which course to follow

### Origin

German Ambivalenz: Latin ambi-, ambi- + Latin valentia, vigor (from valēns, valent- present participle of valēre, to be strong. See wal- in Indo-European Roots).

### Synonym

__eg__: there is ambivalence over whether cars should be encouraged into the countryside

+ EQUIVOCATION
+ opposite(s): CERTAINTY, DECISIVENESS

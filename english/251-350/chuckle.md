chuckle
### Meaning
+ _vi_: to laugh quietly
	+ __eg__:  She chuckled at the memory

+ _noun_:  She chuckled at the memory
	+ __eg__: She gave a chuckle of delight

### Origin

late 16th cent. (in the sense ‘laugh convulsively’): from chuck meaning ‘to cluck’ in late Middle English

### Synonym

__eg__: Adam chuckled to himself as he drove away

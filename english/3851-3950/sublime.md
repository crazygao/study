+ _adj_: Inspiring awe; impressive
+ _adj_: Archaic. Raised aloft; set high
+ _adj_: Obsolete. Of lofty appearance or bearing; haughty

+ _noun_: Something sublime
+ _noun_: An ultimate example

+ _vt_: To render sublime

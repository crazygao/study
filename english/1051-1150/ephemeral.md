ephemeral
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Lasting for a markedly brief time
	+ __eg__: “There remain some truths too ephemeral to be captured in the cold pages of a court transcript” (Irving R. Kaufman). 
+ _adj_: Living or lasting only for a day, as certain plants or insects do.

+ _noun_: A markedly short-lived thing

### Origin

[From Greek ephēmeros: ep-, epi-, epi- + hēmerā, day.]

### Synonym

__eg__: fashions are ephemeral

+ TRANSITORY
+ opposite(s): LONG-LIVED, PERMANENT

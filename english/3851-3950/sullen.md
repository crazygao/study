sullen
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Showing a brooding ill humor or silent resentment; morose or sulky
+ _adj_: Gloomy or somber in tone, color, or portent
    + __eg__: sullen, gray skies
+ _adj_: Sluggish; slow
    + __eg__: the sullen current of a canal

### Origin

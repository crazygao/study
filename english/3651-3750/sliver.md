sliver
### Meaning
+ _noun_: A slender piece cut, split, or broken off; a splinter
    + __eg__: slivers of broken glass
+ _noun_: A small narrow piece, portion, or plot
    + __eg__: a sliver of land
+ _noun_: A continuous strand of loose wool, flax, or cotton, ready for drawing and twisting

+ _vt & i_: To split or become split into slivers

gust
### Meaning
+ _noun_: A strong, abrupt rush of wind
+ _noun_: A sudden burst, as of rain or smoke
+ _noun_: An outburst of emotion

+ _vi_: To blow in gusts

### Origin

[Probably from Old Norse gustr. See gheu- in Indo-European Roots.]

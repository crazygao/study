slither
### Meaning
+ _vi_: To glide or slide like a reptile
+ _vi_: To walk with a sliding or shuffling gait
+ _vi_: To slip and slide, as on a loose or uneven surface
+ _vt_: To cause to slither

+ _noun_: A slithering movement or gait

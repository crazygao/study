extol
### Meaning
+ _vt_: To praise highly; exalt

### Origin

[Middle English extollen, from Latin extollere, to lift up, praise : ex-, up from; see ex- + tollere, to lift; See tel…ô- in Indo-European Roots.]

### Synonym

__eg__: nutritionists have long extolled the virtues of rice

+ PRAISE ENTHUSIASTICALLY
+ opposite(s): CRITICIZE

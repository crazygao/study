spoof
### Meaning
+ _noun_: Nonsense; tomfoolery
+ _noun_: A hoax
+ _noun_: A gentle satirical imitation; a light parody

+ _vt_: To deceive
+ _vt_: To do a spoof of; satirize gently

### Origin

After Spoof, name of a game invented by Arthur Roberts (1852-1933), British comedian

### Synonym

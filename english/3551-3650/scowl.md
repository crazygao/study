scowl
### Meaning
+ _vi_: To wrinkle or contract the brow as an expression of anger or disapproval
+ _vt_: To express with a frowning facial expression

+ _noun_: A look of anger or frowning disapproval

### Origin

Middle English scoulen, probably of Scandinavian origin

### Synonym

__eg__: she stamped into the room with a scowl on her face

+ FROWN
+ opposite(s): SMILE, GRIN

__eg__: she scowled at him defiantly

+ GLOWER
+ opposite(s): SMILE, GRIN, BEAM

bonnet
### Meaning
+ _adj_:  a hat tied with strings under the chin, worn by babies and, especially in the past, by women
+ _adj_:  (BrE) (NAmE hood) the metal part over the front of a vehicle, usually covering the engine

### Origin

late Middle English (denoting a soft brimless hat for men): from Old French bonet, from medieval Latin abonnis ‘headgear’. Sense 1 dates from the late 15th cent

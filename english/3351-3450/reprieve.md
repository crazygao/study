reprieve
### Meaning
+ _vt_: To postpone or cancel the punishment of
+ _vt_: To bring relief to

+ _noun_:
   1. Postponement or cancellation of a punishment.
   2. A warrant for such an action
+ _noun_: Temporary relief, as from danger or pain

### Origin

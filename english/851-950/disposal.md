disposal
### Meaning
+ _noun_: the act of getting rid of sth
	+ __eg__: a bomb disposal squad
+ _noun_: (business) the sale of part of a business, property, etc
	+ __eg__: The next step is to think about disposal of your assets
+ _noun_: = ↑waste-disposal unit
	+ __eg__: Idiom: ↑at your disposal

### Synonym

__eg__: the ageing planes are earmarked for disposal

+ THROWING AWAY

__eg__: we have twenty copies of this promotional album for disposal

+ DISTRIBUTION

__eg__: the disposal of the troops in two lines

+ ARRANGEMENT

__eg__: they may appeal against the decision of the Panel in the disposal of a case

+ SETTLEMENT

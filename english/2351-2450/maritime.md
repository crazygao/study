maritime
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Of, relating to, or adjacent to the sea
+ _adj_: Of or relating to marine shipping or navigation
+ _adj_: Of or resembling a mariner

### Origin

Latin maritimus, from mare, mari-, sea. See mori- in Indo-European Roots

importune
### Meaning
+ _vt_: To beset with insistent or repeated requests; entreat pressingly
+ _vt_: Archaic. To ask for urgently or repeatedly
+ _vt_: To annoy; vex
+ _vi_: To plead or urge irksomely, often persistently

+ _adj_: Importunate

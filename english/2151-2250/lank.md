lank
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Long and lean
+ _adj_: Long, straight, and limp
    + __eg__: lank and floppy hair

### Origin

Middle English, from Old English hlanc

### Synonym

__eg__: the man had lank, brown, greasy hair

+ LIMP
+ opposite(s): GLOSSY, LUSTROUS

__eg__: his long, lank figure

+ lanky.

squabble
### Meaning
+ _vi_: To engage in a disagreeable argument, usually over a trivial matter; wrangle

+ _noun_: A noisy quarrel, usually about a trivial matter

### Origin

Probably of Scandinavian origin

### Synonym

__eg__: there was a squabble over which way they should go

+ QUARREL

__eg__: the boys were squabbling over a ball

+ QUARREL

projectile
### Meaning
+ _noun_: A fired, thrown, or otherwise propelled object, such as a bullet, having no capacity for self-propulsion
+ _noun_: A self-propelled missile, such as a rocket

+ _adj_: Capable of being impelled or hurled forward
+ _adj_: Driving forward; impelling
    + __eg__: a projectile force
+ _adj_: Zoology. Capable of being thrust outward; protrusile

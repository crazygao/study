morsel
### Meaning
+ _noun_: A small piece of food
+ _noun_: A tasty delicacy; a tidbit
+ _noun_: A small amount; a piece
    + __eg__: a morsel of gossip
+ _noun_: One that is delightful and extremely pleasing

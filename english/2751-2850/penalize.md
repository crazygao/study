penalize
### Meaning
+ _vt_: To subject to a penalty, especially for infringement of a law or official regulation
+ _vt_: To impose a handicap on; place at a disadvantage

### Synonym

__eg__: he promised to penalize parents whose children missed school

+ PUNISH
+ opposite(s): REWARD

__eg__: an enterprise culture penalizes those at the bottom of the income pile

+ HANDICAP
+ opposite(s): FAVOUR, HELP

__eg__: Section 18 penalizes the possession of a firearm with intent to commit a crime

+ PROHIBIT
+ opposite(s): LEGALIZE

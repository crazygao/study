maelstrom
### Meaning
+ _noun_: A violent or turbulent situation
    + __eg__: caught in the maelstrom of war
+ _noun_: A whirlpool of extraordinary size or violence

### Origin

Obsolete Dutch : Dutch malen, to grind, whirl (from Middle Dutch. See mel…ô- in Indo-European Roots) + Dutch stroom, stream (from Middle Dutch. See sreu- in Indo-European Roots).

### Synonym

cacophony
### Meaning
+ _noun_: a mixture of loud unpleasant sounds
	+ __eg__: despite the cacophony, Rita slept on

### Origin

mid 17th cent.: from French cacophonie, from Greek kakophōnia, from kakophōnos ‘ill-sounding’, from kakos ‘bad’ + phōnē ‘sound’.

### Synonym

__eg__: despite the cacophony, Rita slept on

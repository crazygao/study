prod
### Meaning
+ _vt_: To jab or poke, as with a pointed object
+ _vt_: To goad to action; incite

+ _vt_: A pointed object used to prod
    + __eg__: a cattle prod
+ _vt_: An incitement; a stimulus

### Origin

Origin unknown

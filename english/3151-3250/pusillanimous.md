pusillanimous
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Lacking courage; cowardly

### Origin

Middle English pusillanimus, from Late Latin pusillanimis: Latin pusillus, weak, diminutive of pullus, young of an animal; See pau- in Indo-European Roots + animus, reason, mind; See an…ô- in Indo-European Roots.

### Synonym

__eg__: the President's increasingly pusillanimous stance on social issues

+ COWARDLY
+ opposite(s): BRAVE, FEARLESS, PLUCKY

savvy
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Well informed and perceptive; shrewd
    + __eg__: savvy Washington insiders

+ _noun_: Practical understanding or shrewdness
    + __eg__: a banker known for financial savvy

+ _vt & i_: To understand; comprehend

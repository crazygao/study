knave
### Meaning
+ _noun_: An unprincipled, crafty fellow
+ _noun_:
   a. A male servant.
   b. A man of humble birth
+ _noun_: Games. See jack

### Origin

[Middle English, from Old English cnafa, boy, male servant.]

### Synonym

__eg__: (archaic) don't let yourself by hoodwinked by that knave

ken
### Meaning
+ _noun_: Perception; understanding
    + __eg__: complex issues well beyond our ken
+ _noun_:
   1. Range of vision.
   2. View; sight

+ _vt_: To know (a person or thing).
+ _vt_: To recognize
+ _vi_: To have knowledge or an understanding

### Origin

[From Middle English kennen(influenced by Old Norse kenna, to know), from Old English cennan, to declare. See gn≈ç- in Indo-European Roots.]

vapid
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Lacking liveliness, animation, or interest; dull
    + __eg__: vapid conversation
+ _adj_: Lacking taste, zest, or flavor; flat
    + __eg__: vapid beer

### Origin

Latin vapidus

### Synonym

__eg__: tuneful but vapid musical comedies

+ INSIPID
+ opposite(s): LIVELY, COLOURFUL, EXCITING

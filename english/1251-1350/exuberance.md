exuberance
### Meaning
+ _noun_: The quality or condition of being exuberant
+ _noun_: An exuberant act or expression

### Synonym

__eg__: the wild exuberance of the dance

+ EBULLIENCE
+ opposite(s): GLOOM

__eg__: the exuberance of the bougainvillea flowers

+ LUXURIANCE
+ opposite(s): MEAGRENESS

obstruct
### Meaning
+ _vt_: To block or fill (a passage) with obstacles or an obstacle
+ _vt_: To impede, retard, or interfere with; hinder
    + __eg__: obstructed my progress
+ _vt_: To get in the way of so as to hide from sight

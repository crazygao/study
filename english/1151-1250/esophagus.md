esophagus
### Meaning
+ _adj_:(= oesophagus) The muscular membranous tube for the passage of food from the pharynx to the stomach; the gullet

### Origin

[Middle English isophagus, from Medieval Latin Ä“sophagus, from Greek oisophagos, arbitrary medical coinage perhaps from ois-, future tense stem of pherein, to carry + -phagos, food (unattested sense) (from phagein, to eat. See bhag- in Indo-European Roots).]

purlieu
### Meaning
+ _noun_: An outlying or neighboring area
+ _noun_: purlieus Outskirts; the environs
+ _noun_: A place that one frequents

### Origin

Middle English purlewe, piece of land on the edge of a forest, probably alteration (influenced by Old French lieu, place), of porale, purale, royal perambulation from Old French porale, from poraler, to traverse : por-, forth (from Latin pr≈ç-. See pro-1) + aler, aller, to go; see alley1

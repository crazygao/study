sporadic
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Occurring at irregular intervals; having no pattern or order in time.
+ _adj_: Appearing singly or at widely scattered localities, as a plant or disease
+ _adj_: Isolated; unique
    + __eg__: a sporadic example

### Origin

Medieval Latin sporadicus, scattered, from Greek sporadikos, from sporas, sporad-. See sper- in Indo-European Roots

### Synonym

__eg__: we braved the sporadic showers | sporadic fighting broke out

+ OCCASIONAL
+ opposite(s): FREQUENT, REGULAR, STEADY, CONTINUOUS

sly
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Adept in craft or cunning
+ _adj_: Lacking or marked by a lack of candor
+ _adj_: Playfully mischievous; roguish
    + __eg__: ZZB

### Origin

ZZC

+ ROGUISH

__eg__: she took a sly sip of water

+ SURREPTITIOUS

__eg__: is she meeting some other guy on the sly?

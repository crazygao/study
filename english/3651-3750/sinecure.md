sinecure
### Meaning
+ _noun_: A position or office that requires little or no work but provides a salary
+ _noun_: Archaic. An ecclesiastical benefice not attached to the spiritual duties of a parish

### Origin

From Medieval Latin (beneficium) sine cūrā, (benefice) without cure (of souls) : Latin sine, without + Latin cūrā, ablative of cūra, care; see cure

### Synonym

__eg__: Connie's job was a sinecure that could be done by anyone

+ EASY JOB

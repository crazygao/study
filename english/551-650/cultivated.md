cultivated
### Meaning
+ _adj_: (of people) having a high level of education and showing good manners
	+ __eg__: a cultivated young woman
+ _adj_: (of land) used to grow crops
	+ __eg__: cultivated fields
+ _adj_: (of plants that are also wild) grown on a farm, etc. in order to be sold
	+ __eg__: cultivated mushrooms

### Synonym

__eg__: a remarkably cultivated man

+ CULTURED
+ opposite(s): IGNORANT

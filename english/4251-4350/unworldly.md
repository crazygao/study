unworldly
### Meaning
+ _adj_: Not of this world; spiritual
+ _adj_: Concerned with matters of the spirit or soul
+ _adj_: Not wise to the ways of the world; naive
    + __eg__: “This now is the story of an unworldly young scientist” (John Simon).

### Synonym

__eg__: the transformation from gauche, unworldly girl to dignified lady

+ NAIVE

__eg__: the region has a stark, unworldly beauty

+ UNEARTHLY

__eg__: a completely unworldly religious order

+ NON-MATERIALISTIC
